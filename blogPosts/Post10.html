Network Analysis of Associations Between Psychological Attributes

Visualization by Tamer Khraisha, Center for Network Science - Central European University (Budapest)
Research Idea and Analysis by Srebrenka Letina, Center for Network Science - Central European University (Budapest)
Data from My Personality Project, led by Michal Kosinski and David Stillwell

Nodes are personality traits and links are the correlations between them. Correlation indicates the linear association, negative (dashed)
or positive (solid), that one personality trait has with another.

Click on each node to see adjacent nodes and a description of the trait
Click on edges to see the value of the correlation between two traits
